Transportation

Evanston is 17 miles from O’Hare Airport, approximately 35 minutes by cab. Midway Airport is 27 miles away from Evanston, and the trip takes approximately 50 minutes by cab. Evanston is also accessible by the CTA subway (the “L”) and the Metra train. Both trains stop at the Davis Street stop, which is walking distance to the Orrington Hotel and the Hilton Garden Inn.

Information on both trains can be found at the Chicago Transit Authority and Metra websites. Shuttles are also available to both O’Hare and Midway Airports.
